## Projects

  • linux-wallpaper-rotator

    Cycles my desktop wallpaper on a systemd timer, fairly across every folder I point it at — works on GNOME, KDE, XFCE, Cinnamon, MATE, LXDE and feh. No sudo.

  • pixflat-theme

    My tweaked take on the RaspberryPi OS PiXflat look — theme and matching icons for LXDE, XFCE and GNOME.

  • xterm-color-schemes

    My XTerm setup, plus a handy cheat-sheet of classic terminal color schemes to copy from.

  • gnome-terminal-profiles

    A fully-offline installer for 26 GNOME Terminal color profiles, each paired with a matching themed font.

  • recalbox-samba-mount

    Mounts a network share so Recalbox can load ROMs straight off the LAN — no copying needed.

  • recalbox-rpi4-composite-configs

    How I got clean composite video out of a Raspberry Pi 4 into an old CRT for Recalbox.

  • ubuntu-look

    Turns a plain Debian GNOME desktop into something that looks and feels like Ubuntu, right on first boot.
